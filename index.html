What is Turo-Turo?

A Filipino cafeteria-style dining experience where you point to what you want

"Turo-Turo" literally means "point-point" in Tagalog. It's a beloved Filipino dining concept where you simply point at the dishes you'd like to try from our display of freshly prepared home-style Filipino favorites.

We specialize in Silog meals - the quintessential Filipino breakfast combination featuring garlic fried rice (sinangag), a perfectly fried egg (itlog), and your choice of savory protein. These hearty, flavorful dishes are enjoyed any time of day!
